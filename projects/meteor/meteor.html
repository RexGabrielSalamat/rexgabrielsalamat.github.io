Meteor Impact Simulator

A physics-based simulation developed for the NASA Space Apps Challenge to assess planetary risk.

Technical Summary

The system estimates affected areas and casualties by processing speed, angle, and meteor composition data through a Python backend, displayed via a responsive web interface.

Role & Collaboration

Lead developer for the repository and integration. Collaborated with a multidisciplinary team to translate complex physics into accessible web visualizations.
